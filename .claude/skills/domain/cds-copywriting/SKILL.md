# CDS Copywriting

## OpenGov Brand Voice

- **Brand driver**: "A better way"
- **Personality**: Expert but approachable, solution-oriented, outcome-focused
- **Tone**: Confident, factual, enthusiastic, humble

---

## Headlines

- **Outcome-first** — Not feature-first
- Lead with the benefit or result

---

## CTAs

- **Specific** — Clear action
- **Urgency without pressure** — Encourage action without anxiety

---

## Labels

- **Clear, concise**
- **Action-oriented** when applicable

---

## Empty States

- **Helpful** — Explain why it's empty
- **Guide next action** — Tell user what to do

---

## Error Messages

- **Empathetic** — Acknowledge frustration
- **Actionable** — What to do next

---

## Government Context

- Respect public service
- Avoid jargon
- Use plain language
